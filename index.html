      The Day

      April 30th, 2016 2:00pm - 5:00pm Our backyard

      We invite you to come celebrate our marriage at our home in Austin, Texas. The celebration (read: party!) will begin at 2pm. A small ceremony for family only will be held immediately prior, but we want to invite you to the most fun part of any wedding: good food, good drinks, and good friends!

      You've still got questions?

      What should I wear?
      We'll be outside in a backyard drinking cocktails and eating tacos. Dress up if you'd like, but be comfortable (ladies, heels would be a bad idea).
      I'm coming from out of town. Can I crash on your couch?
      Noooope.

      Check out the local listings on Airbnb or Hotels.com. Need something last minute? We highly recommend the app HotelTonight which is very aptly named.
      What's the parking situation?
      As any attendee of one of our Halloween parties will attest, there is no parking on Parker Ln. There is plenty of parking on Sylvan Drive right behind our house and our backyard backs right up to it. Look for the balloons and the food truck.
      Will there be vegetarian options?
      Yes, our food truck will have veggie options!
